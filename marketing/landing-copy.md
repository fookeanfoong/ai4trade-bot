- 🧮 **Sized to you** — tell it your capital and daily goal; it suggests how much to put in.
- 🕗 **Pre-open, not stale** — refreshed before the bell so you're not trading yesterday's view.
- 📱 **Installs like an app** — add to your home screen, works offline.
- 🔍 **Transparent by default** — we log the record honestly, wins *and* losses.

### How it works (3 steps)
1. **Set your numbers** — capital available + how much you'd like to make (a target, not a promise).
2. **Open before the bell** — see today's tickers with exact buy / stop / target prices.
3. **You decide** — place the trades in your own broker. The app never touches your money.
